# Retail Sales Performance Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to understand how the business is performing beyond just revenue.

At first glance, the company appears to be doing well with strong sales across products, categories, and cities. However, a deeper analysis reveals that high revenue is not translating into meaningful profit.

The goal of this project is to identify key issues affecting profitability and provide practical, data-driven recommendations.

---

## Business Problem

The company is focused on increasing sales, but lacks visibility into profitability.

Key challenges:

* High revenue but extremely low profit margins
* Heavy discounting without understanding its impact
* Lack of clarity on profitable vs loss-making products
* Inconsistent performance across cities
* No tracking of high-risk (loss-making) transactions

---

## Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Google Colab

---

## Key Insights

* Revenue is consistently high across categories (~₹2B+), but profit margins are extremely low (0.01%–0.02%)
* Nearly 48% of transactions are loss-making
* Discounts above 10% significantly reduce profitability and often lead to losses
* High-discount transactions resulted in losses of over ₹215 million
* Products like Shoes and Shirt generate high revenue but are loss-making
* Laptop is the most profitable product across categories
* Delhi is the most profitable city, while Mumbai is operating at a loss

---

## Key Findings (Human Perspective)

The business is clearly performing well in terms of sales, but not in terms of profit.

In many cases, the company is selling products at a loss due to aggressive discounting. Some of the top-selling products and cities are not contributing to profitability, which creates a hidden financial risk.

This shows that focusing only on revenue can be misleading if profit margins are not tracked properly.

---

## Recommendations

* Limit discounts to a maximum of 10% to maintain profitability
* Shift focus from revenue to profit margin optimization
* Promote high-margin products like Laptop
* Re-evaluate pricing for loss-making products like Shoes
* Implement city-specific pricing strategies
* Monitor high-risk transactions (high discount + loss) regularly

---

## Conclusion

This project highlights the importance of focusing on profitability rather than just revenue.

Even small improvements in profit margin can lead to significant gains because the revenue base is already very high.
