# Project Summary

This project analyzes retail sales data to uncover why high revenue is not translating into profit.

## Key Highlights

* Revenue exceeds ₹2B per category, but margins are near zero
* ~48% of transactions are loss-making
* Discounts above 10% lead to negative profit
* Products like Shoes generate high sales but consistent losses
* Delhi is profitable, while Mumbai operates at a loss

## Outcome

Identified pricing and discount inefficiencies and recommended margin-focused strategies to improve profitability.
